Risky Text of Data Leakage Detection in Privacy Policy Using BERT


People tend to accept privacy policies without reading them when they create new accounts or enable new services of these applications. We created an application using deep learning model BERT to help people better understand the privacy policies more efficiently and know the privacy risks involved.
